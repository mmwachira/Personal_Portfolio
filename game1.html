FlipnFind

FlipnFind is a casual puzzle game designed for players of all ages. Flip cards to reveal the hidden images and match each image with its counterpart to score points. Each level presents you with new images to match with each level becoming harder and harder. Kick back and solve some puzzles as you relax.

Source code can be found at FlipnFind
